        This issue has been open for 4 years now, and it has gotten pretty much out of control.

        There are over 1700 posts in here, and with the GitHub interface being what it is, it is absolutely impossible to find anything, even when you know what you need to look for. Its time to close it.

        If you have a specific issues with getting the cameras to work, please open a dedicated issue on the issue tracker!

        For the general discussion, the news sharing and so on, we have created discussion topics, one for each IPU generation and one for general discussion.

        • Camera support (IPU3) #1352
        • Camera support (IPU4) #1353
        • Camera support (IPU6) #1354
        • Camera support (General) #1355

        For example, news about the upstreaming status of IPU6 should be posted in the IPU6 topic, while news about libcamera integration into userspace should be posted in the general topic.

        I repeat, PLEASE post actual BUGS on the ISSUE TRACKER and not in the discussion topics.

        If you have any suggestions about this "structure", feel free to hit me up on Matrix.
